Welcome to
Cafe Andreatico

At Cafe Andreatico, we are more than just a coffee shop â€“ we are the heart and
soul of the vibrant community in front of City College of Calamba. Our commitment
to exceptional coffee and warm hospitality creates a haven for students, locals,
and coffee enthusiasts alike.

Cafe Andreatico was born out of a shared passion for great coffee and a desire
to foster a sense of togetherness. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create
a welcoming space where people could savor outstanding coffee, engage in meaningful
conversations, and find a place to call their own.
